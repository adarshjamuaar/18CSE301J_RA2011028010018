About the Datasets:

1. GDP 2011Q1-2022Q3: This dataset contains multiple GDP contributing factors and their contribution through the quarters.

2. Crop Production: This dataset has multiple parameters including Crops, Seasons, States, District, Production and Yield. These parameteres are helpful to study the Agriculture situation in the country and any necessary changes which can be brought up to make the sector prosper.

1. Tableau

Both GDP and CropProduction Datasets have been used to produce different Visualizations with year being a connecting factor for the two datasets.


2. Gephi

The figure below is generated using Gephi. Two new dataset was extracted from the dataset "CropProduction" and the datasets "Node" and "Edge" was created. The "node" dataset contains "id" and "label" where label represents crops and seasons. The "edge" dataset contains "source", "target" and weight" where source is the crop name and target is the season name. Weight describes the number of districts farming the crop in a particular season.
